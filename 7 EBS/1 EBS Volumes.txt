EBS Volumes:
=============

	- EC2 machine will lose the root volume when it gets terminated.
	- EBS volumes are a network drive that you can attach to your instances while they run.
	- Allows instance to persist data.
	
	- Its a network drive
	- It can be detached and attached to an instance very quickly
	- Locked to an AZ
	
	
	
	Types:
	
		GP2 - SSD
		IO1 - SSD
		ST1 - HDD
		SC1 - HDD
		Magnetic 
		
	EBS volumes are characterized in Size | Throughput | IOPS
